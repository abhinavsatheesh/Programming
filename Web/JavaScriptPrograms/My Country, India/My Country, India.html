About India

India is a democratic and republic country that found its independence on 15th August 1947. In India, the president and prime minister rule the country. The first prime minister of India was Jawaharlal Nehru. The first president of India was Dr. Rajendra Prasad. India is known for its democracy. There are lots of mountains, rivers, plains etc, and it is not only about the landforms. The people are united. This helped India to fight the British, who came to rule us for over 200 years.
